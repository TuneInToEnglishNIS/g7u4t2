'Let it snow, let it snow, let it snow' by Frank Sinatra

BEFORE LISTENING

Take a few minutes to write down your most memorable experiences with snow, whether positive or negative. Then, share your memories with a partner or a small group. Finally, discuss how snow memories relate to the idea of “Let It Snow.”
